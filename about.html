About Us

Precision Manufacturing Co. (PMCO) is a trusted export and global sourcing partner based in Goa, India. With over 30 years of experience, we empower Indian MSMEs to go global while helping international buyers source high-quality, compliant, and cost-effective products from verified Indian suppliers.

We bridge the gap between global demand and Indian manufacturing excellence — offering end-to-end services including supplier identification, quality inspection, logistics, compliance, and on-ground support.
